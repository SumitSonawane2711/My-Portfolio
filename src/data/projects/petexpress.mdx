## Project Overview

PetExpress is a digital pet-care rewards platform that connects pet parents with local pet-care businesses such as veterinary clinics, grooming centers, boarding facilities, pet stores, and other service providers.

The platform enables users to discover businesses, earn loyalty points through visits, redeem rewards, manage pet profiles, and stay updated with pet-related content. It provides dedicated portals for pet parents, business partners, and administrators to manage their respective workflows while maintaining a seamless user experience.
