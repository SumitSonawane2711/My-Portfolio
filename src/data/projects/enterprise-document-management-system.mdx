## Project Overview

The Enterprise Document Management System is an enterprise web application designed to streamline how organizations manage enterprise documents throughout their lifecycle. The platform centralizes HR records, simplifies document workflows, and enables secure storage, retrieval, and management through role-based access control.

Currently under active development, the system focuses on improving operational efficiency, reducing manual paperwork, and ensuring secure handling of enterprise records across the organization.
